Gotcha Pork Roast

Description

Made by Soma Yukihira from Food Wars, recreated by Tumblr user, realanimefood

Ingredients

  • 3 packages of bacon
  • 6 potatos
  • 0.5 cup mushrooms (minced)
  • 1 small onion (minced)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 0.25 cup red wine
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sweet sake
  • rosemary
  • butcher twine
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350-370F
  2. Heat some oil (or butter) to a pan, sautee the onions and mushrooms
  3. Wash and cut the potatoes into chunks then steam until soft. Remove and peel the skin off when cool enough to handle.
  4. Mash the potatoes, leaving some chunks. Add in the sauteed mushrooms and onions then mix it all together
  5. Form the mixed potato into a cylindrical shape then drape the bacon over the potatoes covering the surface completely.
  6. Place your bacon wrapped potato into a baking dish along with some roasemary and pop it into the oven for four hours.
  7. For the sauce, on medium heated pan, add butter, soysauce, sake and wine let it cook for about 10 minutes and set aside until the pork is ready.
  8. Once the pork is done, drizzle the sauce on and it's ready to be served!
